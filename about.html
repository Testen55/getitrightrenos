About Get It Right Renovations Inc.

More than a renovation — it's an education in building right.

Our Story

Get It Right Renovations Inc. is a Toronto‑based, Black‑owned, union‑affiliated construction company proudly serving Toronto and the GTA. Founded on principles of craftsmanship, sustainability, and integrity, we specialize in high‑quality, energy‑efficient, and code‑compliant renovations.

We are not a cheap handyman service. We fix structural, moisture, and performance problems properly — not cosmetically. Every project reflects our commitment to doing it right the first time.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔ Licensed & Insured Professionals – Full coverage and peace of mind on every project
  • ✔ Union Local 27 Carpenters – Access to the highest standards of craftsmanship
  • ✔ Government Rebate Expertise – Up to $5,600 in savings through rebate qualification & guidance
  • ✔ Transparent Pricing – No hidden costs. Detailed written quotes with clear scope definition
  • ✔ Code-Compliant Execution – Every project meets or exceeds Ontario building codes
  • ✔ Sustainability & Durability – Long-term performance and energy efficiency at our core

Our Mission & Values

Mission: We make renovations accessible, affordable, and sustainable. We empower homeowners and businesses to invest in quality, code-compliant work that lasts.

Core Values:

  • Integrity – Honest communication, transparent pricing, no cutting corners
  • Craftsmanship – Union-level expertise with attention to detail
  • Sustainability – Energy-efficient solutions that benefit the environment and your wallet
  • Community – Supporting Local 27, giving back to Toronto & GTA neighborhoods

What We Won't Do

We believe in being upfront about what we offer and what we don't:

  • ❌ We are not the cheapest — we offer the best value
  • ❌ We do not rush jobs — we do them right
  • ❌ We do not hide problems — we address them
  • ❌ We do not do cosmetic flips — we build lasting solutions
