About Get It Right Renovations Inc.

More than a renovation — it’s an education in building right.

Our Story

Founded by Matthew Martin, Get It Right Renovations Inc. is a unionized (Local 27 Carpenters), Black-owned renovation company proudly serving Toronto and the GTA. We specialize in sustainable, energy-efficient upgrades that improve your home and save you money through government rebates.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔ Licensed & insured professionals
  • ✔ Members of Local 27 Carpenters Union
  • ✔ Expertise in government rebate programs (up to $5,600 savings)
  • ✔ Transparent quotes & financing options
  • ✔ Sustainability & craftsmanship at our core

Mission & Values

We make renovations accessible, affordable, and sustainable. Integrity, craftsmanship, sustainability, and community guide every project.
